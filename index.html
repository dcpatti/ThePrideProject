The Pride Project


Every summer, most Major League Baseball teams celebrate their LGBTQ+ fans, staff and community by hosting a Pride-themed game. Basic productions may include a special-ticket promotional item; more elaborate events include pregame speakers, special anthem singers, and in-game entertainment features.



The Pride Project analyzes fan responses to the Pride Night promotions and events, in an attempt to highlight and understand inclusiveness in fandom.

Pride Night on Twitter

We analyzed over 10,000 tweets about MLB Pride Night to evaluate inclusiveness and identify traits among the teams whose Twitter feeds generated the most positive or negative sentiments.
